Featuring Thalmic Myo

Bringing visual learning to kids for all of their educational needs. Many students struggle with understanding things at a conceptual level. Using visual learning, we can help kids solve this problem, while also making it fun and entertaining for them by using the latest and greatest technology.

Featuring Leap Motion

You have heard of kids learning with hands-on experience, how about with fingers on experience. With Simplimy, kids can utilize a dynamic duo combo pair of the Thalmic Myo and Leap Motion to solve a variety of educational problems and learning the fundamental process of problem solving along the way.
